About

I always said I would write a book. But, let's face it. Who the fuck would read it? Do people still read books? I don't even know how to create and publish a book... So, I thought. Fuck it, I'll make a website. People love looking at websites. Not sure if anyone really reads anything on them (Apart from facts of Facebook). I guess I'll find out.
